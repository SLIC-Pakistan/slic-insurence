Introduction to SLIC

The State Life Insurance Corporation of Pakistan (SLIC) is a state-owned entity that plays a pivotal role in the life insurance sector in Pakistan. Established with the purpose of providing life insurance services and promoting financial security, SLIC is the largest and one of the most trusted life insurance companies in the country.

Benefits of an insurance plan from us:

Government Backing: Being a state-owned corporation, it is seen as a reliable and stable option for life insurance.
Wide Range of Products: It offers a diverse portfolio of life insurance products to meet the needs of different customers, from individuals to large groups.
Affordable Premiums: SLIC products are often more affordable compared to those offered by private companies, making life insurance accessible to a larger segment of the population.
Accessibility: With a large network of agents and branches, as well as digital platforms, SLIC ensures that its services are accessible across Pakistan.

Additional Benefits of Choosing SLIC

History and Mission of SLIC

SLIC was founded in 1972 with the goal of providing affordable and accessible life insurance coverage to the citizens of Pakistan. Over the decades, the corporation has grown to become the country’s most trusted life insurance provider. The company’s mission is to provide financial protection to the people of Pakistan, empowering them to plan for a secure future. The company is committed to innovation, customer satisfaction, and responsible business practices, all of which help it maintain its dominant position in the industry.

Our Insurance Plans

State Life Insurance Corporation offers a range of life insurance plans to suit various needs. Below are some of the popular plans we offer:

  • Life Term Insurance Plan: Provides coverage for a specified term, offering a lump sum payout in case of the policyholder's death during the term.
  • Whole Life Insurance: A lifelong coverage plan that ensures financial protection for your family no matter when you pass away.
  • Endowment Plan: Combines life coverage with an investment feature. Pays a lump sum amount upon policy maturity or in case of the policyholder's death.
  • Family Income Plan: Provides monthly payouts to the beneficiaries of the policyholder in case of death, along with a lump sum amount after maturity.
  • Unit Linked Insurance Plan (ULIP): A combination of life insurance and investment opportunities, where premiums are invested in equity, debt, or balanced funds.
  • Health Insurance Plans: Comprehensive coverage for medical expenses, including hospitalization, surgeries, and critical illness treatments.
